The Adoption Problem

90% of products fail not because they lack features, but because they fail at adoption. Amazing capabilities go unused. Powerful solutions get abandoned. The gap between what we build and what users actually adopt has never been wider.

Traditional product development treats adoption as an afterthought—something to worry about after launch. ADPD flips this model entirely. We design for adoption from day one, thinking about real-world usage before writing a single line of code.

Introducing ADPD

Adoption-Driven Product Design, Development & Delivery is a revolutionary methodology that ensures every product decision is filtered through the lens of user adoption. It works across any industry and transforms how we think about building software.

The FUSE Framework

Four gates every product must pass before users will adopt it

F - Findability

Can users discover your solution when they need it? If they can't find it, nothing else matters.

U - Understandability

Do users immediately grasp what your product does and why they should care?

S - Shippability

Is your product reliable, secure, and performant enough for real-world use?

E - Ease of Use

Can users achieve their goals with minimal effort and maximum delight?

MAS (Minimum Adoption Score): 5/10 - The minimum threshold to proceed

TAS (Target Adoption Score): 7+/10 - Ready for mainstream adoption
